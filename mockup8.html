I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Maryland. Before joining UMD, I was a Predoctoral Research Assistant at the Becker Friedman Institute at the University of Chicago, and a Research Assistant at the Laboratory for Effective Anti-Poverty Policies (LEAP) at Bocconi University and at BRAC Bangladesh.

I am interested in development economics and behavioral economics, with a focus on gender, poverty, and field experiments in the Global South.
